Work Practices/team descriptions

Kudos to griffins riding unicorns...or a guide to Vets.gov teams

Dragons: personalization

Mission: build and maintain experiences that contribute to a unique personalized experience for Vets.gov users based on their personal data.

Dragons Products

  • Profile
  • Account
  • Dashboard
  • PCIU Phone Fetch + Update
  • PCIU Email Fetch + Update
  • Update Address (Vet360)
  • Update Phone Number (Vet360)
  • Update Email Address (Vet360)
  • User Notifications
  • Document Center
  • Eligibility Requirements Catalogue
  • Beta product launches

Griffin: identity

Mission: build and maintain experiences that improve and expand authentication and authorization on Vets.gov, as well as maintain Facility Locator, GI Bill Comparison Tool, and Health Tools.

Griffin Products

  • Secure Messaging
  • Manage Your VA Health Care Online
  • Prescription Refill
  • Access VA Health Records using VA Blue Button
  • Upgrade to MHV Premium
  • DS Logon Option
  • Login Flow
  • MHV Login Option
  • Authentication and Authorization
  • Facility Locator
  • SSO / shared session across VA properties
  • Dependents Login Option

Nebula: sitewide

Mission: build and maintain experiences that affect user’s overall interaction with Vets.gov (i.e. experiences that are cross-product) as well as the content throughout the site.

Nebula Products

  • Merger-related products!
  • User Feedback Flow
  • Design System
  • Site Banner Alerts
  • Welcome Kit
  • Discharge Upgrade Tool
  • Scorecard
  • Playbook
  • Analytics
  • Header / Footer
  • All static content
  • Homepage
  • Crisis Line modal
  • Information Architecture
  • Search

Rainbows: benefit modernization

Mission: build and maintain experiences that improve Veterans experience applying for benefits and services.

Rainbows Products

  • Form Pre-fill
  • Burial Benefits Application (21P-530)
  • Pension Benefits Application (21P-527EZ)
  • Veteran ID Card Application
  • Pre-Need Burial Benefits Application (21P-530)
  • Voc Rehab & Employment Benefits Application (VAF 28-1900)
  • Voc Rehab & Employment Counseling Application (VAF 28-8832)
  • Education Benefits Application (22-1990)
  • Education Change of Program or Training Application (22-1995)
  • Education Benefits Transfer Application (22-1990E)
  • Education Benefits for National Call to Service Application (22-1990N)
  • Education Benefits Application under FRY or DEA (22-5490)
  • Education Change of Program or Training Application under FRY or DEA (22-5495)
  • Save in Progress
  • Health Care Application (10-10 EZ)
  • Dependent Benefits Application and Modification (21-686c, 21-674)
  • GI Bill Complaints / Feedback

Unicorns: benefit modernization

Mission: build and maintain experiences that improve Veterans experience dealing with disability compensation and managing their various benefits.

Unicorns Products

  • Application authentication upgrade to JWT
  • Application migration to AWS (from AITC)
  • Disability Comp Benefits Application (21-526 EZ)
  • Intent to File Submission (21-0966)
  • Private Medical Information Release (21-4142)
  • Auto Allowance and Adaptive Equipment Application (21-4502)
  • PTSD Benefits Application (21-0781, 21-0781a)
  • Aid and Attendance Benefits Application (21-0779, 21-2680)
  • Unemployability Benefits Application (21-8940)
  • User-uploaded doc exposure
  • Mock Data Tool
  • Appeals Status
  • Claims Status
  • Document upload
  • VA Letters and Documents
  • PCIU Comp & Pension Address Fetch + Update
  • Post-9/11 GI Bill Benefit Information

DevOps

Mission: Maintain a stable, secure, and high performing site, establish reusable libraries, patterns, and processes for use across Vets.gov, and support build, launch, and platform-level maintenance of new products.

DevOps Products

  • Downtime Notification System
  • Vets.gov Infrastructure
  • Integration + Deployment Pipeline
  • SOCKS Proxy
  • Security
  • Performance
  • Environment connectivity
  • Load Testing
  • Monitoring
  • On-call rotation management
  • Backend service connectivity
  • Incident response
  • Sentry
  • Grafana
  • Jenkins
  • Prometheus
  • Access control and management
