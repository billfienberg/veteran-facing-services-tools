Work Practices/README

How We Work

This directory contains information on how we do things as a team. Process. Culture. Excitement. You know.

We have an Org Chart for reference, and everything we do is governed by a code of conduct.

We use GitHub for just about everything, so please ensure you are familiar with our best practices for using GitHub and contributing code. "Code" also means documents, images, and any files that can be put under source control.

Teams

  • Design Team Resources - Resources primarily by and for the Design team.
  • Engineering Team Resources - Resources primarily by and for the Engineering team.
  • Product Management Team Resources - Resources primarily by and for the Product Management team.
  • Rainbows Delivery Team Resources - Resources primarily by and for the Rainbows delivery team, which consists of designers, engineers, and product managers.

People and Processes

  • Onboarding and Offboarding Resources - Resources for people joining the team, leaving the team, and notes left behind by those who have left the team.
  • How to Make Purchase Requests

Other Useful Documents

  • Testing Things on Staging – User accounts and instructions for trying things out.
  • Discovery Toolkit - Resources to use during the Discovery Sprint process.
  • How to File Bugs, Tasks, and Questions
  • Team-wide Mailing Lists - Who is on which lists, and addresses to email team-wide lists
  • How to add people to DSVA Slack - Process for adding new members or guests to DSVA Slack or particular channel(s).
  • How to add people to Github - Process for adding new members to Github.
