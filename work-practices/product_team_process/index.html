This space aims to be a source of information for new and existing members of the project about common processes (e.g. how tos for confluence and JIRA) and guidelines for visual design, user research, software development, and information security.

Product Request Process After releasing product and software, the teams need a single way to track issue requests - new feature ideas as well as defects. With this form Vets.gov stakeholders, Vets.gov Call Center and others can submit a request to add something new or ask that something not working be fixed. Once you complete the form, email it to XX. The teams will then triage and if applicable place in priority order on their backlogs.
